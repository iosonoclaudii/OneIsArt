Maldera Claudio

Claudio Maldera was born on 09/26/96 in Italy. At the age of 5 he developed a passion for art and information technology; by combining these passions he managed to realize a dream. He studied accountancy and computer programming at school and graduated in 2015. During this period he developed new passions, such as graphics and video editing. His knowledge starts from drawing, graphic designing and video editing; He is now working as full-stack developer.
